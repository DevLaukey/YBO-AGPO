Our Course

Introduction to Tendering

This class is spearheaded by our team of trainers dubbed the YBOA Trainers. Our trainers are esteemed experts in the field of Entrepreneurship.

Limited, Partnership and Sole Proprietorship Registration

This class will comprehensively engage in assisting our members and youths in the entire Nation to incorporate and register their own enterprises into either registered businesses.

Basic Business Management Skills

Once our trainees have owned either registered business names or limited companies, our able Team of Trainers(TOT) trains these young entrepreneurs on key business management skills.

Procurement Laws, Circulars and Directives

Before venturing into the world of tendering, we always impart our trainers with key knowledge on how to relate with relevant procurement laws as per the Laws of Kenya.

List of Procurement Entities

In this Class, YBOA issues the List of all Public procurement entities to our trainees. This list helps them to know which government department to begin trading.

How to Win a Tender/LPO or LSO

Tendering is a competition and only the best entrepreneur wins. YBOA’s greatest dream is to have all our members winning different tenders from various public and private entities.

Market Survey and Request for Quotation Filling (RFQ Filling)

In this class we train our trainees on the need to carry out a thorough market survey before quoting their prices to the procurement entity.

Tender Documents Filling and Submission

In this level we train our members on how to fill the tender forms found among tender documents. For one to win such a tender he/she must study the tender documents keenly and understand their requirements.

Reassessment of the Tender Results

In this class we take our members through a thorough assessment of their tender results. We take special note on all those who lost in the tendering competition to ascertain why they failed in the competition.

Delivery (Servicing the Tender)

Once a tenderee has won a tender, he/she is expected to service that tender within the days they stipulated in the tender form. Apart from servicing the tender, they are expected to deliver goods in a professional manner.

L.P.O Financings

Given the fact that majority of our members are starters in the field of tenderpreneur, we as YBOA go an extra mile in assisting our needy members to service their tenders.

Follow up on Payment

Once an entrepreneur has delivered the goods/services to the procuring entity, he or she is supposed to be paid in not more than working 30 days.

Investment

The main objective of this class is to aid our members in wisely investing their resources in gainful ventures.
